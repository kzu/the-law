# Definition of Legal Plunder

These principles are developed in [The Law](/the-law.md) and rest on [Natural Rights](/fundamental-principles/natural-rights.md).
The fatal principle that law may organize plunder. Once admitted, every class tries to capture the power to plunder. This page is one of the central hubs of the bundle.

## Key Points

For the foundational treatment, see [the definition of law](/fundamental-principles/law-as-organization-of-defense.md) and [the proper sphere of law](/true-law/proper-sphere.md).
- Plunder made respectable by law. (see legal plunder).
- Leads to universal plunder when the victims seize the legislature. The opposite occurs through the [causes of perversion](/perversion-of-law/causes-of-perversion.md). (see legal plunder).
