# Collective Force

These principles are developed in [The Law](/the-law.md) and rest on [Natural Rights](/fundamental-principles/natural-rights.md).
Individuals may combine their right of defense into a regular common force. See the [central definition](/fundamental-principles/law-as-organization-of-defense.md).

## Key Points

For the foundational treatment, see [the definition of law](/fundamental-principles/law-as-organization-of-defense.md) and [the proper sphere of law](/true-law/proper-sphere.md).
- The common force inherits exactly the same moral limits as individual force.
- It cannot lawfully be used for plunder. The opposite occurs through the [causes of perversion](/perversion-of-law/causes-of-perversion.md). (see legal plunder).

For more, see [legal plunder](/legal-plunder/definition.md).
