# Non-Aggression

These principles are developed in [The Law](/the-law.md) and rest on [Natural Rights](/fundamental-principles/natural-rights.md).
Force has not been given us to annihilate the equal rights of others. See the [central definition](/fundamental-principles/law-as-organization-of-defense.md).

## Key Points

For the foundational treatment, see [the definition of law](/fundamental-principles/law-as-organization-of-defense.md) and [the proper sphere of law](/true-law/proper-sphere.md).
- This limit applies equally to the state.
- Foundation of limited government. The opposite occurs through the [causes of perversion](/perversion-of-law/causes-of-perversion.md). (see Natural Rights).

For more, see [legal plunder](/legal-plunder/definition.md).
